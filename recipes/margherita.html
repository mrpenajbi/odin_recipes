Margherita Recipe

Description

Here you will see a nice and easy recipe to make you dinner guest give you a standing ovation, Don't be shy and timid this recipe has simple ingredients, that can be found in local grocery store.

Make sure to follow these easy steps even a monkey can follow them, and make a great pizza, so get Baking!

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pizza crust or Italian bread shell
  • 3 plum tomato (blank)s plum tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • ⅓ cup BUITONI® Refrigerated Pesto with Basil
  • 1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F. Place pizza crust on baking sheet.
  2. Spread pesto over pizza crust. Arrange tomatoes over pesto. Sprinkle with cheese and crushed red pepper.
  3. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown. Cut into wedges.
